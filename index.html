What is Survival Race about?

Survival Race is a great IO racing game with various cars. Steer your car around to battle with other supercars and collect keys, stars, and coins.

Thrilling races of two cars in Survival Race

After the developer launched this game in December 2022, I tried playing through the game to get a sense of its scope and the differences in game mode design and abilities. This interesting game shines a spotlight on a survival race in which the arena king must push other supercars out and collect coins, keys, and stars. This game makes you think you know where it's going, and then yanks the rug from under you because of collapsing tiles. It can be said that the developer has changed a lot in the game's gameplay compared to other racing games. Would the changes bring the Survival Race game into a whole new twisted light, or would it fall into the shadow of its racing predecessors?

Playable game modes of Survival Race

Practice Mode

The first game mode available in this game is the Practice Mode, which provides you with the opportunity to engage in a free race. In this unique mode, you can freely navigate the hexagon arena, focusing on collecting as many green checkpoints as possible. This mode serves as the perfect training ground for honing your driving skills, allowing you to familiarize yourself with driving controls. Importantly, the race in this mode is endless. It continues indefinitely until your car falls off the edge of the hexagon arena. This feature enables you to practice without time constraints, encouraging you to refine your techniques, experiment with different driving strategies, and become more adept at maneuvering your vehicle. falls out of the hexagon arena.

Battle Mode

Next, we have the exhilarating Battle Mode, which introduces a competitive racing experience between four supercars. At the start of this intense race, you must drive your car toward the center of the arena and crash into other competing supercars. Your goal is not just to race, but to strategically collide with your rivals while maneuvering around the collapsing hexagon tiles that threaten to disappear beneath you. As you race, you will have the chance to collect various power-ups, keys, and coins that can be used to unlock cars or improve your cars.

  • Critical Hit: This powerful boost allows you to obliterate your rivals' cars upon collision.
  • Speedup: Once you collect this power-up, your car's speed will significantly increase, allowing you to outpace your competitors.

Keep in mind that the arena gradually shrinks, adding pressure as the race progresses. The hexagon tiles will disappear after you drive over them, so it's essential to stay agile and avoid falling out of the hexagon arena. Your primary objective is to survive until the end of the race while aiming to earn cups by crashing into and eliminating other cars. The more cups you collect, the higher your rank will climb on the leaderboard.

Parkour Mode

The third game mode is the Parkour Mode, which offers a thrilling challenge where you compete against other supercars to gather as many stars and keys as possible. In this mode, stars are often positioned on elevated and distant platforms, requiring you to skillfully drive your car to jump and reach these challenging locations. The racer who successfully collects the most stars will secure the top position on the leaderboard, making strategic planning and precision driving essential.

Autoball Mode

Finally, we have the Autoball Mode, where you can participate in a unique football match. In this exciting game mode, you and three other supercars must navigate the arena to hit five giant balls. Your objective is to maneuver these balls into one of the four goalposts scattered throughout the arena. Scoring goals is key to achieving the highest rank on the leaderboard, so aim to score as many times as possible. Additionally, the arena is filled with keys and power-ups, so make sure to collect these items as you play to enhance your chances of victory in the football match.

What are the controls for Survival Race?

PC:

If you want to drive the car to steer around, press the A-D keys or the left-right arrow keys,

If you want to control the car to jump, press the W key, the arrow key, the left mouse button, or the spacebar.

If you want to control the car to fly, double-tap the W key, the arrow key, the left mouse button, or the spacebar.

MOBILE:

If you want to steer the car around, hold the screen.

If you want the car to jump or fly, tap the screen once or twice.

GAME CONTROLLER:

Use the directional buttons to drive the car around.

Use the X, A, or B button to control the car to jump or fly.

What are the different vehicles in Survival Race?

This game offers 21 cars with different designs and colors, each featuring unique designs and vibrant colors. This variety not only enhances the visual appeal of the game but also allows you to select vehicles that match your personal style and racing preferences.

Unlocking cars

If you want to unlock cars in the game, you must join as many races as possible or accumulate as many coins, cups, or keys as you can. Here are the unlockable cars in this game.

  • GMA T.50 S: This racing car features exceptional aerodynamics and a lightweight design for unparalleled speed.
  • Lamborghini Essenza SCV12: In fast-paced races, you can choose this car because it features boosted acceleration in straightaways.
  • McLaren Solus GT: You can unlock this car because it features increased grip on high-speed turns, improving handling during sharp corners.
  • Bugatti Bolide: This supercar features turbo boost for short bursts of extreme speed, perfect for overtaking opponents.
  • Aston Martin Vulcan: This racing car has an enhanced braking system that allows for quicker stops and better control.
  • Ferrari FXXK: This supercar features automatic downforce adjustment to improve stability at high speeds.
  • McLaren P1 GTR: This car is famous for its energy recovery system that provides temporary speed boosts during races.
  • Porsche 935: You can choose this supercar because of its high torque output for quick acceleration from a standstill.
  • Ford GT MKII: This car features superior handling dynamics that enhance performance on tight tracks.
  • Brabham BT62: This car is famous for its lightweight chassis and minimalistic design that prioritizes aerodynamics and speed.
  • Ascari A10: This unlockable supercar features a distinctive design that combines elegance with aggressive styling, making it visually striking on the road and track.

Customizing cars

After unlocking all cars in this game, you can select your favorite racing car and dive into a world of customization. The game allows you to personalize your vehicle to reflect your unique style. You can change the color of your car to a variety of options, including vibrant shades like red, orange, yellow, green, blue, dark blue, dark purple, and light purple. This customization feature not only adds a personal touch to your vehicle but also makes your racing experience even more engaging as you showcase your individuality on the track.

What platforms is Survival Race playable on?

You can play Survival Race online on a web browser on https://survivalrace.io. The game is fully optimized for both PC and mobile devices, ensuring smooth gameplay and an engaging experience. Moreover, you can use different game controller devices to control the cars in the game. Whether you are a fan of traditional game controllers or modern wireless options, you can customize your setup to enhance your racing experience. This support for different controllers allows for a more immersive and comfortable gameplay experience, catering to the preferences of casual gamers and serious racers alike.

Differences between Survival Race and other IO games

This game differs from other IO games through several unique features and gameplay mechanics that enhance the overall experience. Here are three key differences between Survival Race and other multiplayer games.:

  • Map: Unlike many traditional IO games, such as Among Us, featuring various maps, Survival Race introduces a dynamic racing environment, which is a hexagon arena made from many hexagon tiles. You can easily get familiar with this map from the first time you play the game.
  • Vehicle Customization: This game features an array of impressive cars, while other multiplayer games, such as LOLBeans io feature characters. Therefore, if you are keen on racing supercars, try playing Survival Race now.
  • Skill-Based Mechanics: While many multiplayer games, such as Worms Zone io, focus on simple mechanics, Survival Race requires players to master various skills, such as precise steering, timing jumps, and leveraging power-ups effectively. This skill-based approach adds depth to the gameplay, making it more challenging and rewarding.

Conclusion

In conclusion, Survival Race should be the strongest multiplayer game this year. With its competitive gameplay and an array of game modes and cars, you can hop on nightly not to unlock various cars, but simply to have fun. As you navigate through the challenges and excitement of this game, you are continually pushed to refine your skills and develop strategies that can lead you to victory. This game is not only about speed, but it is also about survival, competition, and mastering the art of racing in an unpredictable environment. Will Survival Race redefine your expectations of racing games? Only time will tell, but it certainly promises to deliver an exhilarating experience that stands out among its peers.
